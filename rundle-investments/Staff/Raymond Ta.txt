Raymond Ta, CIM
Investment Advisor
Raymond Ta is an investment advisor at Rundle Investments., which he joined in 2017. Prior to joining Rundle, he worked as a financial advisor with CIII Imperial Service providing financial advice on debt/cash management and investment and retiring planning for high net worth clients.
Mr. Ta obtained his Bachelor of Commerce diploma from Red Deer College and is currently completing his Bachelor of Commerce degree from the Haskayne School of Business from the University of Calgary. He has completed the Canadian Securities Course, Conduct and Practices Handbook, Wealth Management Essentials Course, and is currently pursuing a CIM designation.
