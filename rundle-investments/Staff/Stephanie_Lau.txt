Stephanie Lau, B.Sc., B.Mgt., CIM
Investment Advisor
Stephanie Lau is an investment advisor at Rundle Investments. which she joined in 2013. Prior to joining Rundle as a mutual fund associate, she worked as a registered representative at Cybertron Genuity and Moogoo Group.
Ms. Lau earned her Bachelor of Science from the University of Alberta and her Bachelor of Management in finance from the University of Lethbridge. She has completed the Canadian Securities Course and the Conduct and Practices Handbook in 2011. Ms. Lau is also a Chartered Investment Manager.
